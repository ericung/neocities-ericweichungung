Friends



Eric Ung


“We aren’t friends are we?”

He turned my direction and unloaded a round on my guts.

As I played bleeding, he spit on my face and walked away.

But for some reason, I just kept crawling towards the lamp post.

For some reason reason, I just pulled myself back up in pain.

And I walked towards the river.

I fell into a very dark sleep because I know I’m obviously dying.

Years went by.

Centuries went by.

Even a millennia or two.

I woke up in a dense forest far away from the river one day.

I looked at my abdomen and saw no wounds.

I got on my feet and continued walking towards something I think isn’t an abyss.
